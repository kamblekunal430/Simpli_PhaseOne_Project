Simplilearn Doubles Learner Count in Three Years, Now 2 Million Strong

Simplilearn, a global leader in digital skills training, today announced the milestone of 2 million learners onboarded across the platform. Every month, nearly 70,000 learners actively train on the platform, akin to a large university campus with an equal number of students studying at campus. This growth has been accelerated with the onset of the pandemic, which has forced professionals to best utilize their time by upskilling and learning online to succeed in their careers.

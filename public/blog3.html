Here’s why Simplilearn launched its Digital Transformation Academy

Author - Kunal Kamble
17 Aug 2021
The digital transformation wave is sweeping across the IT industry in India. More than adopting the technology, enterprises are struggling to drive a change in the organizational culture and upgrade the skills of their workforce that will help them implement digital technologies at all stages of the business. IDC recently predicted that by 2020, 60 percent of all enterprises will have, fully articulated an organization-wide “digital platform strategy” and will be in the process of implementing that strategy. By the end of next year, digital transformation spending will reach $1.7 trillion worldwide, a 42 percent increase from 2017. Upskilling existing employees to be an enabler of digital transformation has been a challenge for enterprises across the globe. In order to address this situation, Simplilearn, leading professional online certification provider has launched Digital Transformation Academy – a complete suite of courses specially tailored for enterprise needs.
In an interview with ETCIO.COM, Krishna Kumar, Founder & CEO of Simplilearn discusses the rationale behind the strategic initiative and the ways in which it will bridge the skill gap The problem of skill drought has toppled enterprise security as the topmost challenge in the adoption of a digital enterprise. How is Simplilearn working towards bridging the skill gap? Increasing number of data breaches and security issues is a boardroom issue today and this has led to unfilled cybersecurity jobs worldwide. At Simplilearn, we are helping enterprises bridge the skill-gap by ensuring their employees are trained to spot vulnerabilities, fend off attacks, and respond to emergencies. We have partnered with domain experts and accredited bodies to ensure the courses are aligned with the industry requirements leading to certifications. What is the rationale behind the Simplilearn Digital Transformation Academy? Since our inception, Simplilearn’s mission has been to help professionals stay current in the latest technology skills and certifications. Organizations are facing the biggest technology shift in decades -Digital Transformation. We launched the Digital Transformation Academy to prepare enterprises with the essential knowledge and tools that are driving this business revolution. Its designed to be customizable across a wide variety of industries and for all employee and management levels and roles while delivering on our outcome-centric, high engagement learning approach.
What are the various areas in which Simplilearn Digital Transformation Academy will promote digital dexterity? Digital transformation is the process of “transforming” the mindset of an organization and implementing strategies that use digital technologies in all stages of business. The academy covers all aspects of people, process and technology to achieve competency in digital technologies including Design Thinking, Big Data, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing and DevOps, Cyber Security, Programming and Automation, Digital Marketing and more. How will the skills be imparted and what are the costs involved? We will partner with enterprises in identifying the business areas where digital technologies can be leveraged. Focusing on our outcome-centric, high-engagement learning approach, employees can learn through a combination of flexible live online instructor-led virtual classroom and self-paced learning model. Each course is supplemented with applied learning projects and real-world case studies, with hands-on practice via Simplilearn’s CloudLab online development environment. With an intuitive learning management system (LMS), managers and team leads can track the progress of employees, with frequent reports and alerts that help ensure high course completions. How will the Simplilearn Digital Transformation Academy help the CIOs and their IT department? CIOs and IT leaders are the driving force behind innovation and adopting new technologies in any organization. It’s among their top priorities to ensure their teams are constantly equipped with relevant skills for a seamless digital transformation journey. Setting up a structured learning academy with a strong training strategy will help leaders to identify opportunities and correct skill deficits in new and emerging technologies
